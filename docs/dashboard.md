# Dashboard

## Current Status

The integration exposes production-oriented energy sensors and imports historical production statistics.

This is sufficient for partial use with the Home Assistant Energy Dashboard, focused on solar production.

## Currently Exposed Energy Sensors

- `sensor.energy_today`
- `sensor.energy_week`
- `sensor.energy_month`
- `sensor.energy_year`
- `sensor.energy_total`

## Future Dashboard Scope

The roadmap still leaves these areas open:

- Consumption
- Injected energy
- Received energy
- Autoconsumption
- Self-sufficiency
